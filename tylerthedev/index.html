Mary Jessica Photography

Role

Freelance Web Developer

 

Description

This freelance project involved a photographer who needed a portfolio for her photography business. Her needs included content autonomy, a mobile and desktop web presence, galleries for images, a blog, and methods of contact.


I determined that a WordPress site with an out-of-the box theme would be a good starting place. I tailored the theme to meet her creative needs and set up the content for her. Training was given on how to use WordPress to upload photos, add galleries, modify sliders, and adjust copy.

BYUIS High School Programs

Role

BYU Independent Study Marketing

Front-End Web Developer

Description

The two years I spent at BYU Independent Study (BYUIS) as a Web Developer was my introduction to professional web development. This project was the pinnacle of my work at my time there.


The BYUIS High School Programs needed their web presence revitalized. I was placed in charge of design and development for the entirety of the project. I utilized HTML, SCSS, and JavaScript to create a new hub for the High School Programs. I also utilized a team of graphic designers, illustrators, and videographers to help with the content creation for the site.
